Target Audience Description

The target audience of the weatherpredictions.biz website fits the following: Who: the people living in the local area Age: all ages Technology: desktop and laptop computers, tablets, and smartphones Income: all income levels Motivation: To be informed of news, events, and the weather in the local area.

Personas

Persona 1
Occupation: elementary yard duty Age: 30 years old. Motivation for using the site: To know the weather forecast. In order to know what footwear and clothing to use. To know for the students, whether to lead them to the gym or to allow them outside in the playground, courts, and field. Social: An active individual who walks and rides bike to work Technology: Uses a mobile phone and laptop

Scenarios

  1. Is an umbrella/rain coat needed?
  2. What footwear is best?
  3. Will leaving earlier be needed to get to destinations safely?
  4. Are extra layers or cooler clothing needed?
  5. What's the news?
